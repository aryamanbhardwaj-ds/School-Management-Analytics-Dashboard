# 🎓 School Management Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Developed an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution for educational institutions to monitor administration, student performance, attendance, fee collection, and teacher payment operations.

The project transforms raw, relational data into interactive dashboards that provide actionable insights and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor student admissions and enrollments
* Analyze student and teacher attendance trends
* Track fee collection and revenue at risk
* Monitor teacher payroll and pending payments
* Provide centralized KPI reporting for school administrators

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* DAX
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The solution uses a relational dataset consisting of multiple interconnected tables, including:

* Students
* Teachers
* Student Attendance
* Teacher Attendance
* Fees
* Teacher Payments
* Class Schedule
* Admissions
* Subjects

---

## 📊 Dashboards Developed

### 1️⃣ Admin Dashboard

Key KPIs:

* Revenue Collected
* Revenue at Risk
* Total Enrolled Students
* Conversion Rate
* Demo Conversion Rate
* Ghost Students
* Lost Leads
* Payroll Pending

Business Value:
Provides school management with a centralized operational and financial overview.

---

### 2️⃣ Teacher Dashboard

Key KPIs:

* Teacher Attendance Rate
* Average Classes Per Teacher
* Average Revenue Per Teacher
* Subject-wise Teacher Performance
* Total Classes Conducted

Business Value:
Enables monitoring of teaching performance and workforce utilization.

---

### 3️⃣ Student Dashboard

Key KPIs:

* Total Students
* Total Admissions
* Student Attendance Rate
* Fee Collection Rate
* Completed Classes
* Subject-wise Attendance Analysis

Business Value:
Provides insights into student engagement, attendance, and academic operations.
